### Description

Some third-party agents may initially appear malicious but may actually be low-competence and not malicious, and a robust agent should detect and manage that ([Steinhardt et al. 2016](http://cs.stanford.edu/~jsteinhardt/publications/crowdsourcing/paper.pdf), [Tabibian et al. 2016](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1610.07472v2)).
