### Mini Description

Judiciously asking humans to label appropriate examples in order to disambiguate among hypotheses

### Description

Judiciously asking humans to label appropriate examples in order to disambiguate among hypotheses, active learning, can be used when ambiguities are detected at a scale or speed where querying humans is practical ([Settles 2010](http://burrsettles.com/pub/settles.activelearning.pdf), [Hanneke 2014](http://dx.doi.org/10.1561/2200000037), [Beygelzimer et al. 2016](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/6182-search-improves-label-for-active-learning.pdf), [Seung et al. 1992](http://doi.acm.org/10.1145/130385.130417), [Beygelzimer et al. 2009](http://doi.acm.org/10.1145/1553374.1553381), [Christiano 2015g](https://medium.com/ai-control/active-learning-for-opaque-powerful-predictors-94724b3adf06), [Taylor et al. 2016](https://intelligence.org/files/AlignmentMachineLearning.pdf)).
